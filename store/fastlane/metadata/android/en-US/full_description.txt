MineraLog is a privacy-focused, offline-first mineral collection manager for collectors, students, and geology enthusiasts.

Features:
* 300+ mineral reference library with 17 collector-specific fields
* Photo cataloging with QR codes (Normal, UV Shortwave, UV Longwave, Macro)
* Support for mineral aggregates (Granite, Gneiss, etc.)
* AES-256 encrypted database (SQLCipher)
* CSV import/export with smart column mapping
* Encrypted backups (Argon2id + AES-256-GCM)
* Advanced search and filtering
* Bilingual (EN/FR) with 606 strings each
* 100% offline (no internet required)
* No ads, no tracking, no analytics
* Open source (Apache 2.0 license)

Privacy & Security:
- 100% offline - no internet permission
- AES-256 database encryption
- Argon2id password hashing
- No data collection or tracking
- Your data stays on YOUR device

Requirements:
- Android 8.1+ (API 27+)
- ~15 MB storage for app
- Camera (optional, for photos)
