## Production Notes

### Equipment
- **Device**: Pixel 6 or Samsung S21 (clean, modern device)
- **Screen Recording**: Built-in Android screen recorder or ADB screenrecord
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps

### Voiceover
- **Voice**: Clear, friendly, professional
- **Pace**: Not too fast (beginners need time to follow)
- **Tone**: Enthusiastic but not overly salesy
- **Recording**: Audacity, Adobe Audition, or professional studio

### Editing
- **Software**: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve
- **Transitions**: Smooth cuts, no flashy effects
- **Text Overlays**: Highlight key UI elements (e.g., "Tap here")
- **Music**: Optional background music (royalty-free, subtle)

### Publishing
- **YouTube**: Official MineraLog channel
- **Format**: MP4, H.264 codec
- **Captions**: Auto-generated + manual review for accuracy
- **Thumbnail**: Eye-catching screenshot with text overlay
- **Playlist**: "MineraLog Tutorials"
